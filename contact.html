FAQs

What are your store opening hours?

  • Our store globally opens between 9AM - 5PM all PDT time.

How do I sign in to 4 account?

  • Under General Settings, go to Account > Sign in.

  • Please also make sure to have access to your email accounts as we need to verify your email after signing in

Help! My phone keeps turning off by itself?

  • We recommend factory resetting your account by holding power button + volume down button.

  • We also recommend backing up your data every once in a while or turn on auto backup in settings.

  • If you still need help on resolving the problems, feel free to contact us.

How do I cancel my order?

  • You can do so by going under account > orders > cancel orders. You can also call us and refer your order number and we will refund the order for you.

  • Please note that shipped orders can not be cancelled unless refunded.

What is your warranty policy?

  • We provide full refund or replace on problematic orders (broken display, dented bezels).

  • Manufacturer warranty provides protection for all device hardware issues for a full year starting from the purchase date.

  • We do not provide replacement for devices returned with accidental damage.
